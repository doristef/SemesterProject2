Shields & Snakes, The Rules

The Basics

First off you start by choosing your character.
Once that is done a button should appear, and it takes you to the board.

The board consists of 30 spaces, a dice button and 2 players.
Press the dice button to throw the dice and start the game.

First player to the finish mark, on it or past it, Wins!

Trap Spaces

Througout the board are traps, that are randomly placed for each game.
There are 5 different traps, some might help you move forward but others might sent you way back!
If a trap moves you to another trap, the latter won't count.

BEWARE!

Throwing a Six

When a player throws a 6, she will get an extra round.
Except if she gets tangled in a trap then there will be no extra round!
But if the player throws another 6, she will not move at all in that round.
So be careful when wishing for that 6!
